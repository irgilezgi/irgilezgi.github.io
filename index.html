About

I am an Assistant Professor (Profesora Ayudante Doctora) in the Department of Social Sciences at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M). My research focuses on forced migration, migration diplomacy, the everyday politics of migration, and refugee governance, using qualitative methods and extensive fieldwork.

Before joining UC3M in September 2025, I was an Associate Researcher (2024–2025) and Postdoctoral Research Fellow (2022–2024) with the Global Politics and Security Programme at the Swedish Institute of International Affairs (UI). I hold a Ph.D. in Political Science from the University of Gothenburg (2022).
